Who We Are

Athletes Don't Think About Sunscreen. We're Changing That.

theUVproject was built by young people who saw a gap — outdoor athletes spending hours in peak UV with zero sun protection culture. We're not a PSA. We're a movement that meets athletes where they are: on the field, on the court, in the moment.

We believe sun safety shouldn't feel medical. It should feel normal — the same way a helmet or cleats do.
