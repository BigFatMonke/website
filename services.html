Pillar 01

Education That Actually Lands

Most UV education feels like a PSA nobody asked for. We do it differently. Our resources are built for athletes: fast, visual, culturally relevant, and grounded in what actually happens on the field.

  • In-school and in-community workshops
  • Digital content library: videos, infographics, quick guides
  • UV Index explainers tied to real sports scenarios
  • Partner-ready curriculum for coaches and athletic trainers
  • Social campaigns built with athletes, not at them
Bring a Workshop to Us

Pillar 02

Free Sunscreen. No Excuses.

If cost or access is a barrier, we remove it. We distribute high-quality sunscreen directly to outdoor youth sports teams, recreation programs, and community organizations — for free.

  • Direct distribution at practices, games, and events
  • Locker room and sideline drop kits
  • Ongoing supply partnerships with schools and clubs
  • SPF 50+ broad spectrum products only
  • No strings, no sign-ups for recipients

Pillar 03

Start a Chapter. Own the Movement.

theUVproject grows when students lead it. Any high school or college student can launch a local chapter and bring UV education and free sunscreen to their own community.

  • Full chapter launch toolkit and onboarding
  • Dedicated support from the national team
  • Access to our education and distribution networks
  • Real leadership experience and resume-worthy impact
  • A community of student leaders across the country
